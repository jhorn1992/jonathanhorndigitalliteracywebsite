Finding & Evaluating Information

To find information online, you can use a search engine or database. Sometimes you will come across information or websites that have invalid information. Below are a few suggestions on how to evaluate the accuracy of a website:

Who wrote/published the site?

When was the site last updated?

What links connect the site?

Who is the website trying to reach?

What is the site's domain?

Where is the site getting its information?
