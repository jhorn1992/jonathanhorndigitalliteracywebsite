What is Digital Divide?

Digital Divide is the gap between those that access and efficiently use digital tools as opposed to those that lack access or understanding of digital tools.

Some examples of the digital divide are people who can't afford a computer, people who don't know how to use a computer, people who don't have internet access, and people who just don't want to have a computer.
